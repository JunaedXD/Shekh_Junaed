About Me

Hi, I'm Shekh Junaed — a 19-year-old web development enthusiast from Kolkata, West Bengal, India. I’m currently a student, and over the past five months, I’ve been diving deep into the world of web development, learning how to build websites just like the one you’re visiting right now! Creating websites started off as a simple interest, but it quickly turned into a passion and a hobby. I love experimenting with design, structure, and functionality to bring ideas to life on the internet. Whether it’s a personal blog, portfolio, or something entirely new, I enjoy the creative process of turning code into something real and useful. Apart from web development, I’m also deeply interested in Artificial Intelligence and a proud space enthusiast. The future of tech and space exploration fascinates me, and I often spend my free time learning more about these fields. If you’re looking for someone to build a clean, functional, and personalized website—or if you just want to talk tech, AI, or space—I’d be happy to connect. Feel free to reach out!
